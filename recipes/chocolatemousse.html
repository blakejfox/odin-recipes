Chocolate Mousse

A delicious cup of decadent mousse. This recipe will make you go 'yum!'

  • 5 1/4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, coursely chopped
  • 14 ounces cold heavy cream
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1-ounce sugar
  • sweetend whipped cream, for garnish
  1. Place chocolate in a large double boiler and stir until melted
  2. Beat the cream over ice until it forms soft peaks. Set aside
  3. With a mixer, whip eggs to soft peaks. Gradually add sugar and whip until firm
  4. Remove the chocolate and using a whisk, fold in egg whites. When egg whites are incorporates, fold in the whipping cream. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until set
